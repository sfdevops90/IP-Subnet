⬡ What Is Network Sloth?

Network Sloth is a free, browser-based toolkit for network engineers and IT professionals. It started as a simple CIDR calculator and has grown into a proper planning tool — subnet calculator, VLSM scenario planner, DHCP generator, firewall rules builder, and more on the way.

Everything runs entirely in your browser. No backend, no server, no sign-up. You enter a network range, the sloth does the maths, you get your subnets. That's the whole deal.

⬡ Our Principles

  • 01 Free, always. Network Sloth will always have a free tier. The tools are funded by non-intrusive advertising, not paywalls or freemium subscriptions.
  • 02 No backend. Everything runs client-side in your browser. Nothing you type into a subnet field or VLAN editor is ever sent to a server.
  • 03 No fluff. Tools designed for engineers who know what they want. No tutorial wizards, no onboarding flows, no "book a demo" popups.
  • 04 Multi-platform output. Wherever possible, config output targets real platforms — Cisco IOS, Juniper, pfSense, Windows Server, MikroTik — not generic pseudocode.

⬡ Supported Platforms

Network Sloth currently generates platform-specific configuration output for:

Cisco IOS  ·  pfSense / OPNsense  ·  Juniper JunOS  ·  Windows Server (PowerShell)  ·  ISC DHCP  ·  Kea DHCP4  ·  MikroTik RouterOS

More platforms are added with each release. If your platform isn't listed, get in touch.
