01 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to the site owner.

Network Sloth also uses localStorage — a browser mechanism similar to cookies that stores small pieces of data on your device but is never automatically sent to a server with each request. We use localStorage solely to remember your cookie consent decision.

Cookies and similar technologies can be categorised as:

Essential  Required for the site to function. Cannot be disabled without breaking functionality.

Advertising  Used to serve and personalise advertisements. Only set with your consent.

Third-Party  Set by external services (such as Google) rather than by Network Sloth directly.

03 How Our Consent System Works

On your first visit to Network Sloth, a cookie consent banner appears at the bottom of the screen. No advertising cookies are set before you interact with this banner.

The banner gives you two clear choices:

✓ Accept — You consent to Google AdSense advertising cookies being loaded. Ads will be personalised based on your browsing history. Your choice is saved in localStorage. AdSense scripts are then dynamically injected into the page.

✗ Reject — You decline optional cookies. AdSense scripts are never loaded. No advertising cookies are set at any point. All calculator and planning tools continue to work identically. Your choice is saved in localStorage.

On subsequent visits, your saved preference is read from localStorage and your choice is honoured automatically — the banner will not reappear.

05 Managing Cookies in Your Browser

All modern browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies directly. Instructions for the most common browsers:

🌐
Google Chrome
Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data, or: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data
🦊
Mozilla Firefox
Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Manage Data or Clear Data
🧭
Safari (macOS)
Safari menu → Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data, or: History → Clear History
📱
Safari (iOS)
Settings app → Safari → Advanced → Website Data, or: Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data
🔵
Microsoft Edge
Settings → Privacy, Search and Services → Clear Browsing Data, or: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
🎭
Opera / Brave
Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and other site data (same as Chrome-based browsers)
⚠  Blocking all cookies or clearing all site data may affect your browsing experience on other websites. It will also clear your Network Sloth consent preference, causing the banner to reappear on your next visit.
