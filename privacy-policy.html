Privacy Policy

Network Sloth is a free, browser-based tool for network engineers. We keep data collection to an absolute minimum — most of this page is about Google AdSense cookies, not us tracking you.

Last updated: June 2026
Short version: Network Sloth doesn't collect your personal data, doesn't require an account, and doesn't store anything you enter into the calculator. Google AdSense places cookies for ad targeting. If you're in the UK/EU, a cookie banner will appear on your first visit.

⬡ Who We Are

Network Sloth (networksloth.io) is a free, client-side subnet calculator and network planning tool. All calculations run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to any server operated by us.

For privacy enquiries, contact us at: privacy@networksloth.io

⬡ Data We Collect

Network Sloth itself collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • IP addresses, subnet inputs, and network plans you enter are processed locally in your browser and are never transmitted to us.
  • We do not require registration, login, or any account.
  • We do not use analytics software (such as Google Analytics) that tracks individual users.
  • CSV exports you generate are created and downloaded entirely client-side.
