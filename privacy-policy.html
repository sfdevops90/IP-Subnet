Network Sloth (networksloth.io) is a free, independent website providing subnet calculation and network planning tools. The site is operated by an individual developer based in the United Kingdom.

For any privacy-related enquiries, including requests to exercise your rights under UK GDPR or GDPR, please contact us at: privacy@networksloth.io

03 Data Collected by Network Sloth Directly
✓  Network Sloth does not operate a backend server, user accounts, database, or analytics platform of its own. All calculation tools run entirely in your browser. No calculation data, IP addresses, or usage information is transmitted to or stored by Network Sloth.

The only data stored locally on your device by Network Sloth is a single cookie consent preference, saved in your browser's localStorage:

06 Data Retention

As Network Sloth does not operate its own data storage infrastructure, there is no personal data held by us to delete. Specifically:

GitHub Pages server logs — retained by GitHub in accordance with their data retention policies. Please refer to the GitHub Privacy Statement for details.

Google AdSense cookies — retained and managed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy. Cookie lifetimes vary; most advertising cookies expire within 13 months per Google's policy.

Cookie consent preference (localStorage) — stored on your device only. You can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for networksloth.io, or by clearing all localStorage through your browser settings.
